About us


“Travel makes one modest, you see what a tiny place you occupy in the world.”

Travelkaro is India’s largest online bus ticketing platform that has transformed bus travel in the country by bringing ease and convenience to millions of Indians who travel using buses. Founded in 2006, Travelkaro is part of India’s leading online travel company MakeMyTrip Limited (NASDAQ: MMYT). By providing widest choice, superior customer service, lowest prices and unmatched benefits. Travelkaro has served over 18 million customers.
